## What is Deep Work?
- Activities performed in a state of full concentration and zero distraction.
- It helps you build valuable skills that are difficult for others to replicate.
- It enables you to finish important work faster and with higher quality.

## According to author how to do deep work properly, in a few points?
- Create a distraction free environment.
- Don’t check your phone regularly.
- Social media reduces attention and weakens focus.
- Do 60–90 minute sessions.
- At the end of work, review tasks, plan tomorrow, then switch off completely.

## How can you implement the principles in your day to day life?
- Choose a fixed time.
- Put phone on Do Not Disturb, no notifications, no multitasking.
- Break big tasks into small steps.
- Rewarding yourself when we complete sessions.

## What are the dangers of social media, in brief?
- Constant comparison with others increases stress and sadness.
- Attention span is reduced that decreases productivity.
- Social media is addictive, it makes you keep scrolling.
- There is loss of privacy.
- Cyberbullying and toxicity on social media affect mental health at great extent.
