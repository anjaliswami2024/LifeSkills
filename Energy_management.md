## What are the activities you do that make you relax - Calm quadrant?
- Going for a slow walk while listening soft songs.
- Deep breathing or meditation.
- Watching calming videos.

## When do you find getting into the Stress quadrant?
- When I don’t get enough sleep and feel mentally tired.
- When I have too many tasks at once and then feel overwhelmed.
- I feel pressure when I don’t understand something.

## How do you understand if you are in the Excitement quadrant?
- When I feel energetic and motivated.
- When I’m curious and want to explore more.
- When I start a task on time instead of avoiding it.

## Sleep is your Superpower
- Sleep loss reduces learning ability, lowers reproductive health.
- Sleep also impacts the heart and immune system.
- Deep sleep protects the brain and may prevent memory decline.
- Sleep is not a luxury but a biological necessity. 
- To improve sleep, keep a regular sleep schedule.

## What are some ideas that you can implement to sleep better?
- Go to bed and wake up at the same time every day, even on weekends.
- Keep your bedroom cool for better sleep.
- Avoid alcohol, caffeine, and naps close to bedtime.
- Limit screen time and bright lights before bed.

## Brain Changing Benefits of Exercise
- Exercise improves mood and reduces stress.
- It improves focus and attention.
- It protects the brain from age-related problem.
- Exercise improves memory and learning ability.
- Exercise lowers the risk of dementia and cognitive diseases.

## What are some steps you can take to exercise more?
- Start with short sessions.
- Track your progress.
- Set phone alarms or reminders.
- Choose activities you enjoy like walking, swimming or any sport.
