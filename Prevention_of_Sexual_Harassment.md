## What kinds of behaviour cause sexual harassment?

- Lack of respect for personal boundaries
- Gender stereotypes
- Disregard of consent
- Misuse of power dynamics
- Influence of alcohol or substance abuse

## What would you do in case you face or witness any incident or repeated incidents of such behaviour?
- Speak up or intervene if it is safe to do so
- Report the incident to the appropriate authority
- Document details like time, place, people involved, and what happened
- Ensure immediate safety
- Set clear boundaries
- Support the affected person and listen without judgment
- Organizations must take complaints seriously and ensure confidentiality
