### Grit
- In longterm, Consistency is better than talent. Instead of instant success, focus on steady growth. Believe that abilities and skills can improve with effort.

### Introduction to Growth Mindset
There are two ways of thinking:
- Fixed mindset — where you believe you either can do something or you can’t and fear failure
- Growth mindset - where you believe in effort, learning, and see failures as opportunities to improve.

### Understanding Internal Locus of Control
- What is the Internal Locus of Control? 
It’s the belief that your actions, choices, and effort determine your outcomes, rather than luck, fate, or fixed traits.
- What is the key point in the video?
Keep focus on putting efforts and hardwork not on talent, this way there is a better chance to grow.

### How to build a Growth Mindset
- Don't avoid tough tasks.
- Don't depend on talent, put efforts.
- Avoid negative self-talk.
- Take feedback as guidance not criticism.
- Be around people who encourage you to grow.
