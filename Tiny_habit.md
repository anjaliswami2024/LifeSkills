### How can you use B = MAP to make making new habits easier? What are M, A and P.
B = MAP means that a Behavior happens only when three things come together at the same moment
- M (Motivation) : Your desire to do the behavior.
- A (Ability) : How easy the behavior is to do.
- P (Prompt) : reminds you to do the behavior.

### Why it is important to "Shine" or Celebrate after each successful completion of habit?

- Creates positive emotion and memories.
- When the action feels rewarding, you are more likely to repeat it.
- It creates momentum.

### Write about the book's perspective on how to make a habit easier to do?
- Focus on small, simple actions.
- Increase friction for behaviours you want to avoid.
- Prepare things in advance.
- Remove obstacles that make the habit difficult.

### Write about the book's perspective on how to make a habit harder to do?
- Remove obstacles from your environment.
- Make the habit feel less enjoyable or less appealing.
- Add extra steps so the habit takes more effort to do.
